## FAQ Section

**Do I need an account?**
No. The extension works completely offline with zero account required. Create an account only when you want cloud sync or access to the companion dashboard.

**Where is my data stored?**
Locally in your browser via Chrome's built-in storage. Premium users can optionally enable cloud sync — your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

**What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
Free gives you up to 5 projects, 14-day history, and all three timer modes. Pro unlocks unlimited projects, full history, CSV, Excel & PDF export, cloud sync, and advanced analytics.

**Can I export my time entries?**
Yes, with a Pro plan. Export to CSV, Excel, or PDF filtered by project and date range — formatted and ready for client invoicing.

**Does it work offline?**
Entirely. The extension uses browser storage and Chrome alarms — no internet connection is ever required. Cloud sync runs in the background when connectivity is available.

**Is there a mobile app?**
Work Timer is a Chrome extension today. A companion web dashboard is available for Pro users at w-timer.com for analytics and entry management on any screen.
