## Long Description (~300 words)

You tracked six hours yesterday. Or was it seven? You're not sure — and neither is your invoice.

Work Timer fixes the gap between working and knowing where your time went. It's a free Chrome extension that lives in your toolbar. Click once to start tracking. Click again to stop. That's it.

No account required. No server. No data leaving your machine. Everything is stored locally in your browser, fully offline.

**Three ways to track:**

- Stopwatch — Start, pause, resume. Your project and description carry over automatically.
- Manual entry — Log time retroactively by entering a time range or typing a duration. Perfect for meetings you forgot to track.
- Pomodoro — Structured 25/5/15 focus sessions with automatic break transitions and sound notifications.

**Organize and analyze:**

- Color-coded projects and tags for clean categorization
- Daily goal progress bar so you know when to stop (or keep going)
- Weekly view with 7-day grid and daily totals
- Statistics with bar charts, pie charts, and monthly heatmaps
- Idle detection — if you step away, choose to keep or discard the idle time

**Your data, your rules:**

Everything stays in your browser by default. When you're ready, create a free account to unlock:

- Cloud sync across all your devices
- PDF, Excel, and CSV export for invoicing
- Unlimited projects and tags
- Advanced analytics and earnings reports
- Team workspaces with timesheet approval workflows

**Plans:**

- Free — 5 projects, all timer modes, no account needed
- Pro — $1.99/mo (or $17.99/yr) for unlimited everything + cloud sync + export
- Team — From $29/mo for up to 10 members with approval workflows and admin controls

Work Timer is built for freelancers, developers, designers, and agencies who want accurate time data without the overhead. Install it now — you'll have your first entry logged in under 10 seconds.
